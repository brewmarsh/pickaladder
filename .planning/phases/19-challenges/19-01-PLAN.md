| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-19-01-01 | Tampering | Credit Balance | mitigate | Use Firestore transactions for all balance updates; enforce server-side validation of wager vs balance |
| T-19-01-02 | Spoofing | Challenge Actions | mitigate | Verify `g.user.uid` against challenge participants for all actions (accept/decline) |
| T-19-01-03 | Repudiation | Wager Loss | mitigate | Log all credit transactions to a separate `transactions` collection for auditability |
